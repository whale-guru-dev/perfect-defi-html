ABOUT

PerfectDefi

The PerfectDefi (PD) token is the native token for the PerfectSwap platform. PerfectSwap is a decentralized exchange on the Velas blockchain. You will be able to compare it to Uniswao on Ethereum or PancakeSwap on the Binance Smart Chain.

The PD token will be the reward token for the PerfectSwap platform. As users provide liquidity to the PerfectSwap dex, the users will be able to take the lp tokens they receive from providing liquidity and stake it for PerfectDefi tokens. As the PerfectSwap exchange receives trading volume, the PerfectSwap team will utilize the fees collected from all trades to buy back and burn PD tokens on the open market.

Tokenomics

The PD token will start off with an initial supply of 100,000. Over time as users begin staking the supply will gradually raise based upon the emission rate and the APR of the farms in the staking platform. The emission rate will initially be set to 1 and will be lowered and increased based upon the needs of the yield farming platform.

Contract Code

In an effort to be as transparent as possible, the contract code for the PerfectDefi token and the masterchef for the PerfectSwap farm will all be publicon explorer.velas.com.

Fair Launch

PerfectDefi was launched without any presale. Anyone who buys PD can have a peace of mind in understanding that no individual has had an unfair advantage in acquiring the token.

Audit

The PD team takes security very seriously. The PerfectDefi token code will be audited by Certik, a third party cryptocurrency auditing company. This is to ensure that the contracts are safe and run as optimal as possible.

Governance Token

The PerfectDefi (PD) token will also be a governance token. Users who hold the PD token will be able to vote on changes that the PD team should make to improve the quality of the platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

Velas leverages Solana code base and is compatible with the Ethereum virtual machine. This enables the Velas blockchain to be the fasted EVM blockchain with a transaction speed in seconds and transaction fees less than a penny. This enables microtransactions and makes trading more beneficial.

The PerfectDefi team will be leveraging the benefits of the Velas blockchain by creating pegged tokens of all the most popular cryptocurrencies. PSwap will have pegged versions of Velas, Bitcoin, Ethereum, Cardano, and every popular token on the market. We will be making our pegged token through a rebase system to where every 8 minutes the token gets rebased and the price gets pegged to the asset that the Oracle is watching. This can be done due to Velas low transaction fees.

Initially the PD token will be a reward token for the PerfectSwap platform. As time goes by and the PerfectSwap exchange becomes the leading exchange on the Velas blockchain and gets transactional volume in billions the fees generated from this would lead to massive buy backs and price appreciation. Over time the PD team will also look for other use cases such as gaming, NFTs, etc..

Roadmap

Phase 1

Phase one of the roadmap will be to acquire funds from the Velas grant program. Initially we are requesting $400,000 from the velas grant program. These funds will go towards operation cost, development cost of the PD token, development cost of the PSwap exchange, development cost of all the pegged assets (pegged btc, usdt, usdc, cardano, xrp, etc..), and marketing expenses.

Phase 2

Once the grant is acquired we will deploy the PerfectDefi token, PerfectDefi Dex, and all the pegged assets onto the Velas blockchain.Once all the smart contracts are completed for these items we will get them audited by Certik to ensure top notch levels of security.

Phase 3

Once all the contracts are deployed and audited the PD team will begin marketing the PD token, the PSwap exchange, and the yield farming staking platform. By default this will also be direct marketing for the Velas blockchain.
